Video: Tom Brady Ugg Commercial

By Gabriel Yanez in NFL, Now! ⋅ September 13, 2011 at 12:01am ⋅ Post a comment

Whether he just pulled them out of Gisele Bundchen’s side of the closet by mistake is anyone’s guess, but Tom Brady wears UGG boots. After getting outdueled by Mark Sanchez in the playoffs, Brady refused to loose again, so he responded to Sanchez’s GQ photo spread with a pair of fuzzy sheepskin boots.

In UGG’s recent attempt to expand its market to male consumers, UGG Australia has joined forces with the New England Patriots’ quarterback in an ad campaign that tries to merge the masculinity of professional sports and hip-hop music with the stylish image UGG has already established. (video after the jump…)

So when will we start seeing fans tailgating in Foxboro with some cozy UGGs on? Probably not anytime soon, but hey Tom, they look good with your Justin Bieber haircut.
