TGIF: The Best Fantasy Football Seasons of The Last 10 Years

October 24, 2008 at 5:21am in Fantasy, Featured
Pete Rampino writes
Add a comment

I am feeling very opinionated…well everyday. Today though, I just felt like cruising through the magical world of football statistics and thought to myself: “Man, I wish I had him on my fantasy team that year.” Skip ahead several hours of more searching and gear-turning in my head and I have composed what I think are the 10 best fantasy seasons of the last decade.

A few rules for my list include:

  • When a player is chosen, he is chosen only once. Otherwise, the whole list would be LaDainian Tomlinson.
  • Only the last 10 years are chosen because I just didn’t want to pick the best single seasons of all time for each position.
  • I didn’t try to pick an even amount of QB, WR, RB, TE, etc. I am picking the best plain and simple regardless of position. So my list could be all QB’s for all you know.
  • I excluded Tom Brady and Randy Moss from the 2007 season. Oh so controversial. I did this because it was too recent and too obvious to choose. If you can’t handle it then, too damn bad!
