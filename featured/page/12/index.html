Edward Gutleber writes
Add a comment

The Jets needed a lot of help to get into post-season on Sunday, but did very little to help themselves first and foremost, as they lost to the Miami Dolphins 19-17, in Miami.

The Jets finish a very disappointing season at 8-8 (2-6 on the road) and miss out on the sixth and final AFC Wild Card spot.  They needed to beat Miami and needed losses from three other teams that were rivaling them for the sixth seed. They finished the season with three consecutive losses that brought them to 8-8 and elimination from playoff contention. Unquestionably it is a big step backward for a team that reached the AFC Championship Game each of the past two years and for a team that had high, Super Bowl aspirations.
