The Knicks (16-16) have now won eight of their last nine games, while the Mavs (20-12) had their current winning steak stopped at six. Nowitski had a season-high 34 points for Dallas, who after a sluggish start to the season, had been playing championship level of basketball of late, especially on the defensive end.
