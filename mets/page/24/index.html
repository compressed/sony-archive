Not anymore.

Between the Jose Reyes signing (six years, $106 million), the Mark Buehrle signing (four years, $58 million), and the Heath Bell signing (three years, $27 million), they have already committed $191 million to free agents this offseason. Combine this with the aggressive manner in which they pursued Albert Pujols (ten-year offer) and C.J.
